What I Offer:

Therapeutic/Sports Massage

In this type of massage, we will focus on specific problem areas using various techniques. It envolves movement from your part. We will continuously assess and reassess until improvements are observed. Dry needling may be incorporated if I find it may benefit you and if you are comfortable with it.

Deep Tissue/Relaxing Massage

This type of massage involves deep, slow strokes aimed at promoting release and relaxation. I will do most of the work, so you can relax. That makes it ideal for full-body sessions. Sessions of 90 or 120 minutes are recommended for a optimal experience.

Most sessions combine elements of both types, tailored to your needs and preferences. To provide you with the best experience, communication during the treatment is essential. Please let me know what you like and don't like so I can adjust accordingly. After all it is your session.

Who am I?

My name is Tiago, and my fitness journey started later in life. However, I quickly became passionate and excited about health. In Portugal, there were ancient healers known as "Endireitas" who always fascinated me with their ability to help people instantly. According to old sayings, some learned their craft from healing animals, while others inherited their knowledge. They were the manual therapists of old times.

Putting two and two together, I made the decision to delve into hands-on therapy. It all began with a one-day massage course, which then led to a year-long program, followed by various Continuing Professional Development (CPD) courses. From practicing on friends to ultimately launching my own massage business.

And so, the journey continues - here I am, doing what I love!
